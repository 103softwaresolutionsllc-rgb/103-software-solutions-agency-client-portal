1Welcome to 103 Software Solutions

Thank you for choosing us to bring your vision to life. This Welcome Packet is your reference guide throughout the project. It covers timelines, communication expectations, revision policies, and our team contacts.

Please read through this document in full before starting Phase 2 (Onboarding), and keep it handy throughout the project. If you have any questions, we're always one message away.

2Your 5-Phase Project Journey

Every project at 103 follows a structured 5-phase process designed to deliver the best results efficiently.

Phase 1
Discovery
We learn about your business, goals, audience, and vision through our discovery form.
Phase 2
Onboarding
You submit all required content, assets, and materials through your portal checklist.
Phase 3
Production
Our team designs and builds your project. You review and provide feedback via the portal.
Phase 4
Launch
Final checks, go-live preparation, and handover. Your product launches to the world.
Phase 5
Post-Launch
A 30-day support window, then a testimonial and referral request. We celebrate your success together.

3Communication Guidelines

  • All project communication happens through your client portal or via email.
  • Response time: we reply to all messages within 1 business day (M–F, 9am–6pm CT).
  • Use the feedback tool in Phase 3 for design comments — avoid sending feedback in multiple channels to prevent confusion.
  • Urgent matters? Email hello@103software.com with "URGENT" in the subject line.
  • Please consolidate feedback before submitting — multiple rounds of small changes slow things down.

4Revision Policy

Your package includes 2 rounds of design revisions during Phase 3 (Production). Additional revision rounds may be purchased at $150/round. Minor text edits and bug fixes are not counted as revision rounds.

  • Submit all feedback for a round at one time — feedback submitted in batches is treated as one round.
  • Scope changes (adding new pages, features, or functionality not in the original brief) are handled separately as a change order.
  • Once Phase 4 (Launch) begins, major design changes are considered out-of-scope.

5Timeline Expectations

  • Phase 1–2: 1–2 weeks (depends on how quickly you complete the onboarding checklist).
  • Phase 3: 2–4 weeks for initial design + revisions.
  • Phase 4: 1 week for launch preparation and go-live.
  • Phase 5: 30 days post-launch support window.
  • Delays in providing materials (Phase 2) will push back the overall timeline proportionally.
